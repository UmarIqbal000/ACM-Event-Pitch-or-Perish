01 Event Overview

What is Pitch or Perish?

This isn't just a hackathon. It's a startup battlefield — where teams don't just build, they survive. Every decision costs credits. Every hour brings a new challenge. And the judges? They're not just watching.

💡
Idea to Product in 3 Hours
Teams ideate, prototype, and pitch a real startup concept within a 3-hour window. No pre-built solutions. Everything happens live on the day.
💰
Credit-Based Tool Economy
Want to use Figma, ChatGPT, or Claude? It'll cost you credits. Every tool access is a strategic resource decision. Spend wisely or go dark.
⚡
Live Judge Interventions
Judges can cut your credits, trigger market disruptions, or hand you a bonus opportunity — at any time. Adaptability isn't optional; it's survival.
🧭
Mentors & Hourly Reviews
Each team is paired with a dedicated mentor for strategic guidance. Judges evaluate progress every hour — no coasting, no last-minute saves.

02 Event Flow · 15 April 2026

How the Day Unfolds

Pre-Event Phase
Promotions & Registrations
Campus-wide promotion goes live across social media, notice boards, and ACM communication channels. Teams register via Google Form. Final team confirmations are issued before the event date.
9:45 AM – 10:50 AM
Check-in & Onboarding
Teams arrive at the venue. A minimum of 1 representative per team must check in. Mentors are introduced, credits are distributed, and the complete rule briefing takes place.
11:00 AM — Kickoff
🚀 The Clock Starts
Judges address all teams. Twist rules are revealed. 100 credits hit every team's account simultaneously. The 3-hour survival clock begins. No warm-up, no second chances.
11:00 AM – 11:30 AM
Phase 1 · Ideation 💡
Brainstorm & Lock Your Idea
Teams brainstorm, debate, and commit to a startup idea. The CEO leads the vision. No pivoting after Phase 1 — unless a Chaos Event forces it.
11:30 AM – 1:30 PM
Phase 2 · Prototyping 🛠️
Build Your MVP
Teams build a Minimum Viable Product using tools purchased with credits. CTO leads execution. Hourly judging checkpoints keep pressure on. Chaos Events can trigger at any time.
1:30 PM – 2:00 PM
Phase 3 · Pitching 🎤
Present to the Judges
Teams present their idea, prototype, and business model to the judging panel. The CMO leads the pitch. Judges score on innovation, execution, adaptability, and pitch quality. One team survives supreme.

04 Game Mechanics — The Twist Layer

The Rules That Break Rules

💰
Credit System
Every team starts with 100 credits. These are your fuel — spent to access AI and design tools. No credits, no tools. Strategic resource allocation separates the survivors from the rest.
⚖️
Dynamic Credit Changes
Judges hold full authority over your credit balance. At any judging round, credits can be boosted for exceptional performance — or slashed for poor decisions. Nobody's safe.
🧭
Mentor Assignment
One mentor is assigned to each team. Mentors guide strategy, help navigate Chaos Events, and facilitate coordination — but they don't build. Your execution is entirely on you.

11 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior startup experience? +
No! Pitch or Perish is designed for all skill levels. Whether you're a first-time founder or an experienced builder, the event tests your creativity, teamwork, and adaptability — not your resume.
Can I bring a pre-built project? +
No. Everything must be built from scratch during the 3-hour event window. Pre-built solutions, templates, or code snippets prepared before the event are strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification.
What happens if we run out of credits? +
You enter Survival Mode. You lose access to all paid tools (AI, design software, mentors) but can still use free resources like Google Docs. You can earn credits back through mini-challenges and judge bonuses.
How are teams formed? +
Teams register as complete units of 4 members with pre-assigned roles (CEO, CTO, CMO, COO). We do not facilitate team matching — come with your crew ready to compete.
Is there a participation fee? +
Registration details including any participation fees will be announced through official ACM Students Chapter channels. Stay tuned to our social media for updates.
